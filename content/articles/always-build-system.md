                <p class="lead">Continuous improvement through persistent automation. The Always Build system ensures your infrastructure evolves continuously.</p>
                
                <h2>Continuous Evolution Philosophy</h2>
                <p>Static systems decay over time. Dependencies drift. Configurations stale. Knowledge gaps widen. The Always Build system counters this entropy through persistent, automated improvement cycles that continuously enhance your operational infrastructure.</p>
                
                <h2>Core Components</h2>
                <p>The system operates through interconnected modules:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>Dependency Monitor:</strong> Tracks available updates, security patches, and new releases across your technology stack</li>
                    <li><strong>Configuration Validator:</strong> Ensures settings remain optimal and compliant with best practices</li>
                    <li><strong>Knowledge Curator:</strong> Maintains documentation currency and identifies gaps requiring attention</li>
                    <li><strong>Security Scanner:</strong> Continuously monitors for vulnerabilities and compliance issues</li>
                </ul>
                
                <h2>Automation Patterns</h2>
                <p>Always Build operates through configurable automation patterns that balance improvement speed against stability risks. Low-risk updates apply automatically. Medium-risk changes require validation. High-risk modifications escalate for human approval.</p>
                
                <h2>Reporting and Visibility</h2>
                <p>Comprehensive logging enables visibility into system evolution. Regular summaries communicate changes applied, improvements realized, and emerging areas requiring attention. Dashboards provide real-time system health views.</p>
